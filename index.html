Automation Expertise Focused on What Matters

AITECH Automation is an industrial automation service provider based in the UAE, delivering PLC programming, system retrofit, and HMI solutions for industrial clients.

We focus exclusively on automation engineering — ensuring clean PLC logic, stable systems, and operator-friendly interfaces. Electrical installation is intentionally excluded, allowing us to fully concentrate on control systems, software, and automation performance.

Our approach combines proven industrial standards with modern automation practices, especially for Siemens environments.
